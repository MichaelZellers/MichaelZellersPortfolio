"I'm not your average pixel pusher."

Who am I?

I am a creative problem solver! I enjoy taking on all sorts of projects ranging from graphic design to in-depth video production. Ever since I was 13 years old, I've been honing my skills through personal, school, and business projects. I hope that I can further develop my expertise through the work you may hire me for!

Why what I do matters.

Since I like to provide creative solutions to my clients and myself, my work will stand out from the rest! I employ creative strategy to my productions, which is where creativity meets functionality. You may notice that I use the word "creative" a lot. That's because creativity really is the most important element to work in my field! If you're not creative, you're just another brick in the wall.

What are my qualifications?

I currently hold two degrees. One in Marketing, and another in Digital Media production. I also obtained my Adobe Suite Certification during my highschool years! Along with that, I completed the NYS BOCES program for Graphic Media Production, and was admitted to the National Technical Honors Society for my outstanding efforts in the curriculum. What matters most though, is the years of experience under my belt both working for myself and for other organizations who sought out my services.
